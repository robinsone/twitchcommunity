About

Given the vast variety of games out there, we encourage our members to try new things and expand their skills across multiple genres. We believe there is something here for almost everyone, and we're always looking to expand the family to include others like us. Bridge Four has been active in World of Warcraft under the guild name "Achievement", before deciding to expand to other types of games, and incorporate streaming into our daily activities. We do our best to maintain positive interactions with other gamers, which is why we are also very selective in our membership.

If you located us through one of our streamers, please give us feedback on how we're doing. We would love to hear how we are making a positive impact, or how you feel we can improve even more. You can also support our streamers directly by "following" them on Twitch, or just bringing others around here to see what we're about.
